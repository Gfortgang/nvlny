Night Vision Live

Every so often, NVL produces, promotes and executes ticketed live music events. NVL has a proven record of cutting edge talent bookings, tasteful venue selection, quality production value and highly targeted event marketing. Night Vision Live alum include Grammy-nominated producers, international powerhouse DJ’s, viral music sensations and top tier underground talent. Night Vision Live events promise cutting-edge music in a beautiful, intimate setting, and are always a one time only experience.

Night Vision Residency

The Night Vision crew hosts a weekly club night every Thursday at Le Baron Chinatown in New York. With locations in Tokyo, London, and Paris, Le Baron is the ideal environment for our local network of artists, young professionals, music fans and students to meet, mingle and celebrate. Resident DJ’s Lenvi & Don Clark supply the tunes every week, bringing in cutting edge dance music sounds from around the world.
